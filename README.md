A Supermarket Analysis is a data-driven evaluation of a grocery store or retail outlet's operations, sales patterns, and customer behavior. It is one of the most common and practical applications of business intelligence because supermarkets handle thousands of daily transactions across vast, diverse inventories.

When you build a dashboard or perform an analysis for a supermarket (similar to what you are doing in your Super-Market-Analysis repository), you are essentially translating raw transactional logs into actionable business strategies.
